	\setlength{\partopsep}{-1.2mm}{\small%
	Some of the high-profile websites I helped develop during my time at the Interaction Consortium (IC) include:
	
	\begin{itemize}\setlength{\itemsep}{-2mm}\setlength{\itemindent}{-3mm}
	\item\textbf{nfsa.gov.au} (serves an 800,000-item collection)
	\item\textbf{iwc.com} (100,000+ registered users)
	\item\textbf{artgallery.nsw.gov.au} (peak of 100,000+ daily hits)
	\item\textbf{mca.com.au}
	\item\textbf{goplay.nsw.gov.au}
	\item\textbf{curiousworks.com.au}
	\end{itemize}
	
	My main responsibilities on these projects lay in implementing the sites' business logic and database interactions in the Django back-end, as well as designing the initial data models and system architecture. This was complemented with the occassional GNU/Linux server administration and front-end development in JavaScript/HTML/CSS.
	
	\vspace{3mm}
	During this time, I also discovered I had a particular aptitude for diagnosing and fixing vexing bugs, which in turn sparked a burning passion for maintainable code and best practices.
	
	\vspace{3mm}
	In addition, the development practices used at the IC provided me with invaluable experience in Agile methodologies, pair programming, and test-driven development.}
